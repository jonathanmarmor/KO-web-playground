Corporate Partnerships and Individual Sponsorships
7th Street Concerts offers a variety of sponsorship opportunities to corporations, small businesses, and individuals. In addition to demonstrating a commitment to the arts and gaining exposure to a culturally-curious and community-minded audience, sponsors receive a range of benefits including print and online recognition, logo visibility, and on-site signage. Contact Kristin Olson, Executive Director, for more information.
