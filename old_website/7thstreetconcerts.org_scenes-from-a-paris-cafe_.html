Experience the elegance of Paris in a soirée of grace and charm for flute and harp.

On a summer evening in Paris, the streets hum with life—laughter, the clink of glasses, and the shimmer of music drifting from a corner café. Charlotte Symphony stars Victor Wang (flute) and Andrea Mumm Trammell (harp) invite you to wander through this soundscape of a Paris sidewalk and beyond. From impressionist dreams to graceful salon pieces and beloved classics, this intimate program evokes the beauty and cosmopolitan spirit of a night out in the City of Light—right here in Charlotte.

Doors open at 3:30 PM for a pre-concert Happy Hour, followed by a 75-minute concert at 4:00 PM, and a post-concert reception with drinks, tasty bites, and a chance to mingle with the artists. Complimentary parking included.

About the Artists

Victor Wang is Principal Flute of the Charlotte Symphony Orchestra, a position he has held since 2016. He is regularly featured as a soloist with the orchestra and has performed at major summer music festivals including Tanglewood, Colorado College, Texas, Atlantic, Norfolk, and Bard. Originally from Rochester, New York, Victor began his flute studies at the Eastman Community Music School and went on to earn both a Bachelor of Sciences in molecular, cellular, and developmental biology and a Master of Music in flute performance from Yale University, where he studied with Ransom Wilson, Anouvong Liensavanh, and Jake Fridkis. Outside of performance, he contributes to the next generation of musicians as a woodwind section coach for the Charlotte Symphony Youth Orchestras and leads masterclasses for student musicians at universities in the region.

Andrea Mumm Trammell is a distinguished harpist and the Dr. Billy Graham Principal Harp of the Charlotte Symphony Orchestra, also serving as Principal Harp of the Colorado Music Festival. She appears regularly as a soloist and chamber musician and has performed as guest principal harpist with orchestras including the Atlanta Symphony, North Carolina Symphony, and Charleston Symphony. A dedicated educator, Andrea teaches harp at the Eastman School of Music, UNC Greensboro, and Davidson College. She holds a Bachelor of Music and Performer’s Certificate from Eastman and a Master of Music with honors from Northwestern University.
